What is Concession Catering?

Concession catering is a type of food service where a business provides ready-to-eat meals, snacks, or beverages in temporary or semi-permanent setups at events, venues, or high-traffic locations. Think of it as food "on-the-go" for large crowds—it's different from traditional catering because it focuses on quick service and often portable food, rather than formal sit-down meals.

What a Concession Stand Looks Like

Guests eat with their eyes first. This is how our Nairobi concession catering looks on-site: vibrant skirting, clear labeling, hot chafing dishes for mains, candy and snacks up front, and visible condiments so people know exactly what they can grab in seconds.

  • Queue-friendly layout with sneeze guards and chafing dishes so lines move fast even during peak rush.
  • Hot mains on one side, snacks and sweet treats on the other, plus drink tubs nearby for quick upsells.
  • Readable menu board and allergen tags that keep choices clear and boost conversion for every guest.
We keep aisles clear, label everything, and dress the stall to match your brand or theme—perfect for festivals, sports days, school fairs, and corporate family days.

Key Features of Concession Catering

Quick Service

Food is prepared for fast consumption, perfect for crowds on the move.

Portable Items

Burgers, hot dogs, sandwiches, fries, snacks, popcorn, ice cream—handheld and easy.

Flexible Locations

Can be set up anywhere there's permission and a crowd—indoors or outdoors.

Event-Focused

Often seasonal or for special events, fairs, festivals, or markets.

Types of Concession Catering We Offer

Food Trucks

Mobile kitchens that move between events or locations. Fully equipped to prepare and serve a variety of meals on the go. Perfect for festivals, corporate events, and community gatherings.

Stalls & Kiosks

Temporary setups at fairs, stadiums, shopping malls, or outdoor markets. Compact, efficient, and designed for high-volume service.

Pop-Up Catering

Short-term operations at corporate events, festivals, trade shows, or private celebrations. We bring the concession experience wherever you need it.

Beverage Concessions

Coffee, juice, soda, smoothies, or specialty drinks at events. Keep your guests refreshed throughout your event.

Specialty Snacks
