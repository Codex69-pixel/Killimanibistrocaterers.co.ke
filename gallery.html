Service Spotlights

We specialise across several event types. Below are quick differences to help you choose the right service for your needs.

Corporate Catering

Professional, punctual, and audit-friendly catering for businesses.

  • Menu flexibility for large groups and dietary restrictions
  • On-time service & formal plating or buffet options
  • Branded service stations, invoicing and event documentation
  • Experienced teams for conferences, launches and galas

Private Events

Intimate gatherings focused on guest experience and personal touches.

  • Flexible menus for small groups and home events
  • Casual to upscale service styles to fit the occasion
  • Customizable add-ons like live stations and dessert bars
  • Attention to dietary and family preferences

Event Setup & Production

Logistics, rentals and staffing to make any event run smoothly.

  • Full setup: furniture, linen, chafing & service equipment
  • Site management and on-site production teams
  • Coordination with venues and third-party vendors
  • Scalable solutions for both indoor and outdoor events
