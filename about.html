Our Story

The Kilimanibistro Journey

Founded in 2015, Kilimanibistro Caterers began with a simple vision: to elevate event catering in Kenya through uncompromising quality, creativity, and service excellence.

What started as a small team of passionate chefs has grown into one of Kenya's most trusted catering companies, serving prestigious events across the country.

Our journey is built on a foundation of culinary innovation, attention to detail, and a deep commitment to making every event memorable through exceptional food and service.
