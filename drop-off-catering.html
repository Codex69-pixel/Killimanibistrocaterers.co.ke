Drop-Off Catering

Ready-to-serve meals for offices and meetings across Nairobi. We cook in Kilimani and deliver hot, labeled, and on time.

Convenient Drop-Off Catering in Nairobi

Not every office needs full service. We prepare, pack, and deliver food that is ready to serve for meetings, trainings, and daily lunches. Everything is handled in our Kilimani kitchen, then brought to your office or venue on schedule.

Temperature-controlled delivery and sealed packaging keep food fresh. Order individual meal boxes for teams or bulk trays for larger floors; every delivery comes with clear labels and setup notes.

What's Included in Our Drop-Off Catering

Delivery & Packaging

  • Delivery of hot, fresh meals in food-safe packaging
  • Meal kits or bulk containers based on order size
  • Clear serving instructions with each delivery
  • Temperature-controlled delivery vehicles
  • On-time delivery guaranteed

Setup Support

  • Disposable utensils, plates, and napkins available
  • Food warmers and chafing dishes (optional)
  • Minimal setup required by the host

Flexible Ordering

  • Portions planned for small teams or large departments
  • Fast turnaround (24-48 hour notice)

  • Regular delivery schedules for offices
  • Easy order adjustments up to 48 hours before
