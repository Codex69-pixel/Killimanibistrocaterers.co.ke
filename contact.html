Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve in Kenya?

We serve the entire Nairobi metropolitan area and can cater events across Kenya with advanced notice. For locations beyond Nairobi, additional transportation fees may apply.

How far in advance should I book catering services?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for smaller events (under 50 guests) and 4-6 weeks for larger events (50+ guests). For weddings and major corporate events, we suggest booking 2-3 months in advance to secure your preferred date.

Do you provide equipment and setup?

Yes, we provide full setup including tables, chairs, linens, tableware, and serving equipment. Our setup packages can be customized based on your event requirements.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. We can accommodate various dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, and specific food allergies. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions during the booking process.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 14+ days before the event receive a full refund. Cancellations 7-14 days before receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable but can be rescheduled for a future date within 3 months.
