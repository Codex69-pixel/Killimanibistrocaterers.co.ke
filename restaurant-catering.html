What is Restaurant Catering?

Restaurant catering is when a restaurant provides food and beverage services for events or private functions, bringing the full restaurant experience to your chosen venue. Unlike quick-service concession catering, restaurant catering focuses on full-course meals with professional service, making it perfect for formal and semi-formal events where quality and presentation are paramount.

What Our Restaurant Catering Looks Like

See the real setup: polished chafing dishes, garnished mains, and clearly separated courses so guests move smoothly down the line. This is the same Nairobi-standard restaurant catering we deploy for galas, office celebrations, and private dinners.

  • Freshly refilled buffet line with proteins, sides, and vegetarian options—kept hot, labelled, and photo-ready.
  • Balanced menu flow: starters first, hearty mains center, desserts last—optimized for guest satisfaction and speed.
  • Service team on-site to guide guests, manage portions, and maintain cleanliness throughout service.
We can theme linens, labels, and garnish styling to match your brand palette or event decor while keeping the line efficient.

Our Restaurant Catering Options

On-Site Catering

Our team comes to your venue to prepare and serve food fresh. Perfect for events where you want live cooking and the ultimate in freshness and presentation.

Off-Site Delivery Catering

Food is prepared at our restaurant kitchen and delivered hot and ready to your event for self-service or minimal staff assistance.

Buffet Catering

Guests serve themselves from an elegant buffet setup. Ideal for large events, corporate functions, and casual celebrations where variety is key.

Plated Catering

Food is individually plated by our staff and served to each guest. Perfect for formal events like galas, executive dinners, and premium celebrations.

Drop-Off Catering

Pre-prepared meals delivered in professional packaging with minimal setup required. Great for office lunches and casual gatherings.
