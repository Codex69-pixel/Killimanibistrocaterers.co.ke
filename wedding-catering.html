Wedding Catering FAQs

Common questions about our wedding catering services

How far in advance should we book wedding catering?
We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for peak season weddings (June-September, December). For off-season weddings, 3-6 months is usually sufficient. Early booking ensures availability and allows time for multiple tasting sessions.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Absolutely. We regularly accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, and other dietary requirements. We'll work with you to create alternative menu options that are just as delicious as the main menu.
What areas in Kenya do you service?
We cater weddings throughout Kenya with our base in Nairobi. We regularly service Naivasha, Nakuru, Mombasa, Kisumu, and surrounding areas. Destination wedding catering is available with travel arrangements.
